Pleated Insect Net with Honeycomb Blackout Duet

  • Home
  • Mosquito Net for Doors
  • Pleated Insect Net with Honeycomb Blackout Duet

Duet – Pleated Insect Net with Honeycomb Blackout is a dual purpose mosquito net cum window covering (2 in 1). Product launched by Insect Shield in Indian market. Honeycomb Blackout is lined with aluminium foil making excellent blackout blind & insulator of heat. The dual purpose product is used in bedroom windows, home theatre windows, children nursery windows, hospital windows & study windows. Duet – Pleated Insect Net / Honeycomb blackout saves precious space in apartments.

It is also the only window covering in India which ensures complete blackout and also saves electricity expenses. Honeycomb non-woven fabric is of the best quality and is waterproof but it should not be installed in windows which are exposed to rainfall. Cleaning has to be done either using a feather brush or a soft hand brush or a vacuum cleaner. Cleaning should never be done by scrubbing using soap & water. Duet – Pleated Insect net is a very useful product if it is used with precautions as advised by company.
